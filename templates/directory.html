ROC Statistical System Directory



General clearinghouses for Taiwan government datasets include:

  • National Statistics website (English and Chinese)
  • Taiwan Open Data portal (Chinese only)

The compilation and dissemination of Taiwan statistics by the ROC government is decentralized and delegated to a number of departments, agencies, and bureaus. See the National Statistics website for more information. Below is a non-exhaustive list of links to websites of these statistical organizations. Note that not all websites below provide public datasets in English.
