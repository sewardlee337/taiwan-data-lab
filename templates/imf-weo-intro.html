Access Taiwan data from the International Monetary Fund's World Economic Outlook (WEO) database via the menu on the left. WEO data available at Taiwan Data Lab will be periodically updated to reflect updates in the WEO database.

The WEO database contains macroeconomic data series from the statistical appendix of the IMF's World Economic Outlook report. Data are generally available from 1980 to present. See the hompage of the WEO database for more information.
