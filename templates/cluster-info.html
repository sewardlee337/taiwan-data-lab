Additional Information

The Taiwan Cluster Mapping Project is conducted by a research team at the Taiwan Institute for Strategy and Competitiveness (TISC), led by Dr. Janet Tan. Dr. Tan's team has been collaborating closely with Harvard Business School's Institute of Strategy and Competitiveness to ensure that the Taiwan Cluster Mapping Project is methodologically-sound and comparable to similar projects in other countries.
